Final Words..


I hope many of you have enjoyed using Clean Coastlines, to learn about keeping our oceans safe from pollution. The idea of this website is to get people to keep our oceans sustainable, by talking about Sustainable Coastlines and what they are doing in New Zealand. Promoting their cause will give people the opportunity to remove rubbish in oceans. However, keeping oceans sustainable will take time, because there are more rubbish to clean on beaches and our waters.


Otherwise, feel free to go access the contact page to fill in your feedback on the form provided. For more information, go to Google and research Sustainable Coastline's website. Their website will contain many information about what they do.
